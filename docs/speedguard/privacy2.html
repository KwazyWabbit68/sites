Privacy Policy for SpeedGuard

Effective Date: 03/19/2023

Developer Information and Privacy Point of Contact

Kurt Vermote is the developer of SpeedGuard. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the app's data handling practices, please contact us at privacy@speedguard.app.

Personal and Sensitive User Data

SpeedGuard accesses and collects the following types of personal and sensitive user data:

  • Your mobile device's geo location to calculate your speed and average speed while using the app.
  • Your user preferences that you set within the app, such as units of measurement and speed limits.

We do not collect any other personal or sensitive user data.

Data Handling Procedures

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of the personal and sensitive user data we collect. We use this data only to provide the services offered by SpeedGuard. We do not share any personal or sensitive user data with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion Policy

We do not persistently store your geo location information on our servers or on your device. This information is only used within the app to calculate your speed and average speed while using the app. Your user preferences are stored on your device and are not shared with any third parties.

This document serves as the privacy policy for SpeedGuard and is labeled as such.
